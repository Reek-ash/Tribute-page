Timeline

1929

Lata Mangeshkar, born in Indore, Madhya Pradesh in a Marathi speaking Gomantak Maratha family to Master Deenanath Mangeshkar and Shevanti Mangeshkar.

1942

Recorded her first song 'Naachu Yaa Gade, Khelu Saari Mani Haus Bhaari' for Master Vinayak's Marathi movie 'Kiti Hasaal'

1947

Got her first break in playback singing in Hindi movies for 'Majboor' with the song 'Dil Mera Toda'

1950-1960

Recorded some of the most melodious and popular songs of Hindi cinema like 'Aayega Aanewaala', 'Piya Tose', 'Lag Ja Gale', 'Aaja Re Pardesi', 'Ajeeb Dastaan Hai Yeh' and 'Tere Liye'.

1974

Received the first ever 'Lifetime Achievement Award' at the prestigious 'Filmfare Awards'.

1989

Awarded the 'Bharat Ratna', the highest civilian honour of the country.
